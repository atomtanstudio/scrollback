# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Scrollback to be a useful, respectful project for people building and
self-hosting personal archives. Participants are expected to be considerate,
constructive, and welcoming in issues, pull requests, discussions, and other
project spaces.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different experience levels, backgrounds, and constraints.
- Keep feedback specific, kind, and focused on the work.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions before escalating conflict.
- Respect privacy and do not post another person's private information.
- Report security concerns through `SECURITY.md` instead of public issues.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, threats, hate speech, sustained personal attacks, sexualized
language or imagery, deliberate intimidation, and publishing private
information are not acceptable in this project.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or
limit participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
Serious or repeated violations may result in a ban from project spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, contact the project
maintainer privately. Reports will be reviewed with care and handled in a way
that protects the people affected as much as possible.
